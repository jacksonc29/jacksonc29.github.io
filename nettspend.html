timeline

Early Rise & Viral Breakthrough (2022-2023)
Dec 2022: Starts releasing music on SoundCloud.
Mid-2023: "Drankdrankdrank" snippet goes viral on Twitter, introducing him to a wider audience.
2023: Releases mixtape KICKDOOR and singles like "Shine N Peace" (hailed as a best song of 2023 by The Fader & NYT)
Mainstream & Collaborations (2024)
Jan 2024: Featured on Evilgiane's "40" (named a best song of 2024).
Mar 2024: First major festival performance at Rolling Loud.
June 2024: Collaborates with OsamaSon on "Withdrawals".
July 2024: Releases "That One Song" (sampling Deftones).
Late 2024: Bad Ass Fcking Kid* (debut album) released; signs with Interscope Records.
2025 & Beyond (Recent & Future)
March 2025: Turns 18; walks Miu Miu show at Paris Fashion Week.
April 2025: Tours, including Lyrical Lemonade Summer Smash.
August 2025: Drops two-song EP gone too soon.
Upcoming: Working on new music, bridging BAD ASS FCKING KID* and next album early life crisis.
